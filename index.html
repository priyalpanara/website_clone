Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

We never touch any data stored in your database. We only use the Metabase API to retrieve metadata about your database instance. This includes the schema of your database.

Can I try Baselit before purchasing

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for all tiers - no credit card information required!

Does Baselit work with messay databases?

It does! We do some behind the scenes magic so that our AI works with all kinds of messy data.

What kind of support is available for Baselit?

The level of support depends on your chosen plan. All plans include email support with 24 hour response. Growth plan users get quicker response times with Slack support. For enterprise users, we provide a dedicated customer success manager with a 6-hour SLA response time.

which databases do you support?

We support most of the popular databases, and continue to add to the list of supported data sources. If we do not currently support yours, we will build an integration within 2 days.
